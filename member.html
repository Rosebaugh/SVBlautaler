Become a Member

Are you an active person who is interested in learning more about German culture and dance?

We welcome everyone who is interested in German culture to join us for a practice, including families! No previous dance experience required.

W

This is a vigorous form of exercise so you need to be ready to jump, skip, waltz, twirl, and have a great time! Loose comfortable clothes and appropriate shoes are recommended.

We wear traditional Bavarian Tracht or attire during our performances. We are able to lend new members some articles of clothing to get you started! So don’t let a lack of German clothing deter you!


We practice on Sunday’s 1 PM at Immanuel Lutheran Church, 1700 Westport RD, Kansas City, MO normally twice a month.

Drop us an email at kcvorplattler1@gmail.com for more information.
